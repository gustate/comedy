Bio


Gus Tate started telling jokes in Beijing, Shanghai, and Hong Kong, where he opened for visiting comedians like Ari Shaffir, Dwayne Perkins, and Nick Griffin. Winner of the 2014 Beijing Comedy Competition and finalist at the 2015 Hong Kong Comedy Competition, Gus currently lives, works, and tells jokes in New York.

In Hong Kong he performed regularly at TakeOut Comedy and Comedy.HK. He has also performed in Beijing and Tianjin with Comedy Club China, in Shanghai and Suzhou with Kung Fu Komedy, in Guangzhou and Shenzhen with Dr. Panda Comedy, and in the Philippines with Comedy Manila and Comedy Cebu.
