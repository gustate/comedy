Gus Tate was just a funny person from Lexington, Kentucky until 2013, when he sold out and started telling jokes at open mic nights in Beijing. He currently lives, works, and tells jokes in Hong Kong.

Gus was judged the winner of the 2014 Beijing Comedy Competition by three drunk expat writers. He continues to be unreasonably proud of this, despite subsequently becoming a finalist at the 2015 Hong Kong Comedy Competition, judged by a panel of nine totally sober expat writers.

Gus has opened for actual professional comedians like Ari Shaffir, Dwayne Perkins, and Nick Griffin. One time he picked up Tom Rhodes from the airport, but that probably doesn't count and there's no way he'd remember.

In Hong Kong he performs regularly at TakeOut Comedy and Comedy.HK. He has also performed in Beijing with Comedy Club China and The Humor Section, in Shanghai and Suzhou with Kung Fu Komedy, in Guangzhou with Dr. Panda Comedy, and in the Philippines with Comedy Cebu.
